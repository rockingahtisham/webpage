Nihari Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef, boneless, cut into small pieces
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 4-5 green cardamom
  • 2 black cardamom
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • Water, as needed
  • Chopped cilantro and green chilies for garnishing

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot or dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  3. Add the ginger garlic paste and fry for a few minutes.
  4. Add the beef and fry until browned on all sides.
  5. Add the salt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, black pepper powder, green cardamom, black cardamom, cinnamon stick, cloves, and bay leaves. Mix well.
  6. Add enough water to cover the beef and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat to low, cover the pot and let simmer for 2-3 hours, or until the beef is tender.
  8. In a separate pan, roast the wheat flour until browned.
