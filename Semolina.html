Semolina Halwa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup almonds, chopped
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup cashews, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan on medium heat.
  2. Add semolina and fry until it turns golden brown and gives off a nutty aroma. This should take about 5-6 minutes.
  3. Add the chopped almonds, cashews, and raisins to the pan and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add sugar, water, and milk to the pan and stir well to combine.
  5. Cook on medium heat until the mixture starts to thicken and the water is absorbed. This should take about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add cardamom powder and mix well.
  7. Remove from heat and let it rest for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Garnish with chopped nuts and serve hot.
