Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Water

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and add cumin seeds.
  3. Add chopped onions and fry until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger paste and garlic paste and fry for a minute.
  5. Add chicken and fry until it changes color.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes, yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 5-10 minutes.
  7. Add garam masala powder, green chilies, mint leaves, and coriander leaves. Mix well and cook for a few more minutes.
  8. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the chicken mixture.
  9. Add enough water to cover the rice and chicken. Bring to a boil, then cover and simmer on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the water has been absorbed.
  10. Turn off the heat and let it sit for 5-10 minutes before serving.
  11. Garnish with fried onions and chopped mint leaves.
