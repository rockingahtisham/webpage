Shikanjabeen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/2 tsp ginger juice
  • 4-5 mint leaves, chopped
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a pitcher, mix together lemon juice, water, sugar, salt, and black salt until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Add cumin powder, chaat masala, ginger juice, and chopped mint leaves. Stir well to combine.
  3. Add ice cubes to the pitcher and stir again.
  4. Pour the Shikanjabeen into glasses and serve immediately.
